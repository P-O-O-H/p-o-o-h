About

Oregon State is an international public research university that draws people from all 50 states and more than 100 countries. We go wherever the challenges are, push ourselves to the very edge of what’s known and keep going. We are determined to forge solutions. We are diverse and welcoming. We embrace our responsibility to Oregon and the world, building a future that’s smarter, healthier, more prosperous and more just. We see what could be and have worked relentlessly for 150 years to make it so. 

1 of 3 land, sea, space and sun grant universities in the U.S.

2 campuses, 11 colleges, 12 experiment stations, Extension programs in all 36 counties, 200+ academic programs — COUNTLESS IMPACTS.

Corvallis

A beautiful, historic and state-of-the-art campus, Pac-12 sports and boundless possibilities — all in one of America’s best college towns.

OSU-Cascades

Our Bend campus offers 20+ majors, small classes, unlimited outdoor recreation and a vast natural laboratory in Central Oregon.

Hatfield Marine Science Center

Our coastal research hub in Newport offers a variety of opportunities to take classes or pursue experiential learning next to the Pacific Ocean.

Portland

Earn an Oregon State degree in business or education in an online/in-person format — or take professional development courses — in the City of Roses.

Extension

With operations in all 36 counties, the OSU Extension Service provides information, services and expertise to 2.3 million Oregonians every year.
